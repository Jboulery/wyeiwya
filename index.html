Ranking chart

The main part of the visualization tool is the ranking section, which shows you the countries extracted from the database ranked according to a criteria based on user input. We extract nutritional values from the database, and the slider in the top part of the page allows you to give more or less weight to those neutritionnal values in the ranking. The values used for the ranking are mean extracted from all the products registered under one country

World Map

On the bottom part of the page, a world map allows you to see the countries ranked above colored according to their rank.

Radar chart

On the right side, a radar chart allows you to visualize and compare the raw nutritional values of a given country with anoter. To do so, just click on the rectangle beside the desired country in the ranking, and its radar chart will appear on the right.

About the project

What You Eat Is Who You Are


Authors : Raphael Allier | Jeremy Boulery | Valentin Carra

What you eat is who you are ! Ever wonder which country eats the healthiest food ? You're going on a trip to Phillipines and you worry that you won't be able to keep on with your diet ? This data visualization project will give you all the answers.

Based on a database provided by OpenFoodFacts and on this Kaggle topic, we created a D3 visualization that allows you to see which country in the world eats the fattest food or the sweetest one.

The source code of the project is available here.
